SELECT Employee_ID,
	Employee_Name, Department,
	Salary_KSH, Hire_Date,
	Address,
	Contact_Information
FROM employee_details WHERE Department = $P{dep_param}
AND Salary_KSH >= $P{min_sal} AND Salary_KSH <= $P{max_sal}

Above snippet is for the department and salary parameter
